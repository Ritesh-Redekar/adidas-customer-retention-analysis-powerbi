# 📊 Adidas Customer Retention Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Adidas retail customer behavior to understand **customer churn, loyalty engagement, repeat purchases, and customer lifetime value (CLV)**.  

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was developed by integrating customer demographics, transaction history, loyalty program data, and store performance to generate insights that support better **customer retention strategies**.

---

## 📂 Datasets Used
The project combines five datasets:

• Customer Demographics  
• Customer Transactions  
• Store Locations  
• Loyalty Program Data  
• Churn Labelled Customers  

These datasets contain customer profiles, purchase activity, loyalty tiers, store information, and churn indicators.

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
• Microsoft Power BI  
• Power Query  
• DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)  
• Data Modeling  
• Data Visualization  

---

## ⚙️ Tasks Performed

**Task 1 – Data Modeling & Cleaning**  
Cleaned datasets using Power Query, corrected data types, removed duplicates, and built a star schema data model.

**Task 2 – Churn & Retention Analysis**  
Calculated churn rate KPI and analyzed churn across region, income level, shopping channel, and loyalty tier.

**Task 3 – Repeat Purchase Analysis**  
Segmented customers based on purchase frequency into Low, Mid, and High tiers.

**Task 4 – Promotion & Loyalty Impact**  
Analyzed the effect of promotions and loyalty tiers on customer spending behavior.

**Task 5 – Store & Channel Performance**  
Compared transaction values and churn rates across store types and channels.

**Task 6 – Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**  
Calculated CLV and segmented customers into Low, Medium, and High value groups.

**Task 7 – Final Dashboard & Executive Summary**  
Consolidated key insights into an interactive dashboard with strategic recommendations.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

**Page 1 – Data Modeling & Cleaning**  
Shows the data preparation process including Power Query transformations and star schema model.

**Page 2 – Churn & Retention Analysis**  
Visualizes churn rate across regions, income groups, channels, and loyalty tiers.

**Page 3 – Repeat Purchase Analysis**  
Segments customers based on purchase frequency and identifies loyal customer groups.

**Page 4 – Promotion & Loyalty Impact**  
Analyzes promotion usage and loyalty program engagement.

**Page 5 – Store & Channel Performance**  
Compares transaction trends and churn behavior across store types and regions.

**Page 6 – Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis**  
Identifies high-value customers based on spending and membership duration.

**Page 7 – Executive Overview**  
Displays key KPIs such as **Churn Rate, Average CLV, and Repeat Purchase Rate** along with strategic insights.

---

## 📈 Key Insights

• Customer churn rate is approximately **49%**, indicating retention improvement opportunities.  
• **Repeat purchase rate reached 85%**, showing strong engagement among loyal customers.  
• Higher **loyalty tiers generate stronger purchase behavior**.  
• Promotions increase purchase value but do not always reduce churn.  
• High CLV customers represent the most valuable segment.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

• Target high-CLV customers with personalized retention campaigns.  
• Improve loyalty program engagement through tier-based rewards.  
• Strengthen marketing strategies in underperforming regions.
